Arguments

Promotes violence

Vigilante actions can escalate violence as they may provoke those targeted or create conflicts within the community. Vigilante violence might begin with hard tapping, slapping and punching. However it escalates to a more intense extralegal trial or in many cases, unauthorized execution. Therefore, the moral justification behind vigilantism may inadvertently inspire extremist behaviors and undermine the authority of the criminal justice system. In 1851 America, a committee of vigilantes was formed. They had established patrols against fire and theft and deported numerous undesirable persons. It asserted the right to search without a warrant and denied the right of fair trial with legal authorities. It was a group, notorious for hanging its captives after brief trials.

Provokes unlawful behavior

Some legal authorities engage in vigilantism. Not to mention extrajudicial killings or extrajudicial executions. For example, in the infamous campaign of former Philippine president Duterte’s “anti-drug war” made multiple calls on the public to kill drug addicts. These calls could constitute vigilante groups to commit unlawful violence and murder. Duterte has also repeatedly urged the Philippine National Police to target suspected drug users and drug dealers with extrajudicial violence, which could be considered instigating law enforcement to commit extralegal actions or murder leading to thousands of innocent deaths or “collateral damage”. Even promising that said personnels will be protected from any case filed. Back in 2020 Philippines, an incident recorded of the senseless killing of a mother and her son by an off-duty officer over noise complaints had caused an outrage throughout the whole of Philippines. Through this act, the government, Philippine National Police and specifically former president Duterte faced extreme criticism and accused for inciting groups of police and military to act in impunity.

Causes unwanted casualties

Vigilantes may target the wrong individuals or community, leading to wrongful accusations and assault which can further social injustice. Vigilantes often break the law in their efforts to punish alleged accused. Furthermore, self-initiated actions can lead to the escalation of conflicts. Retaliation is a common consequence when one group perceives itself as wronged or threatened. Such escalations can result in a cycle of violence, posing significant risks to public order and safety. Law enforcers are allowed to patrol on the ground as they undergo rigorous training so they may be able to competently apply legal practices to incidents they attend. Therefore, without any professional training, vigilantes may resort to lethal use of force which could do more harm to innocents and damage properties.

Vigilante Violence Disproportionately Harms Marginalized Communities

Poses more detrimental effects to legal authorities

Vigilantism occurs when people lose trust in the government to keep its side of the bargain. If citizens engage in vigilantism and believe they must enforce justice into their own hands, it reflects a lack of trust in law institutions, diminishing cooperation and public trust to authorized personnels. Vigilantes may interfere with ongoing investigations, which may compromise evidence and potentially undermine legal processes. Complicating the roles and responsibilities of law enforcements. Vigilante incidents tend to attract the media’s attention. If known vigilantes face prosecution, legal authorities may face backlash of negative publicity from some local and wider communities.

Counterarguments

1. They might propose establishing an organized citizens on patrol that could assist local law enforcement and maintain public order within a community. Such proposals are certainly welcomed by the community as they would ease the burden on police officers. However, legal authorities cannot be certain that the members of such a committee would adhere to the established guidelines and refrain from unlawful activities. Furthermore, even if that specific committee performs thorough background checks, members could easily omit or conceal their past misdemeanors or criminal behavior. As stated before, without appropriate protocols in place, some individuals may misuse their authority and assert that their actions comply with legal standards. When these situations arise, it is solely the government that will face criticism for being negligent in allowing these committees.

2. They might also suggest that immediate action is required. Even when an event is happening directly in front of a person. It is common sense to seek assistance before intervening impulsively. Assessing the circumstances beforehand could minimize confusion. Otherwise, it leads to worsening the situation.

Rebuttals

One consequence of vigilantism is its potential escalation to anarchy. People resort to vigilantism when they lose faith in the legal system. This loss of faith often originates from perceptions of corruption, ineffectiveness, or an inability to address certain crimes. However, permitting vigilantism would only increase this distrust, as it would indicate that the state cannot effectively deliver justice on its own. When individuals believe they can no longer depend on established systems to safeguard their rights or hold wrongdoers accountable, they may take justice into their own hands which would further diminish confidence towards legal institutions. This situation could trigger a chain reaction effect that results in chaos over time.

The legalization of vigilantism will raise significant moral and ethical concerns. The moral argument against vigilantism is based on the principle that justice must be served fairly, guided by reason and established principles rather than emotional impulses or personal biases. Vigilantes frequently act out of anger or in the belief of their own sense of righteousness which may cloud their judgment. In some instances, vigilantes may justify violent actions based on their individual moral codes. If society permits individuals to act according to their personal values, it risks encouraging an environment where personal values and interpretations of justice take priority over universally accepted legal principles. This ultimately undermines the core purpose of the legal system.
